General Rules

  • You must be 18+ years old to be considered for whitelisting
  • Characters must be 18+ years old.
  • A working microphone of decent quality is required.
  • Respect all players and staff. Any sort of disrespect, harassment or toxicity towards any member of Hive will result in immediate action. This rule will be heavily enforced. No Exceptions. [35 Warning Points]
  • Any form of sexual, racist or other hateful remarks will absolutely not be tolerated. This rule will be heavily enforced. No Exceptions. [35 Warning Points]
  • You must be in the Hive Discord. This is to allow Staff to contact you, if needed. You can join here. You cannot join the server without being on our Discord server.
  • Inappropriate character names are prohibited, they need to be atleast somewhat realistic. This includes names of famous people (Bill Gates etc.). Some exceptions can be applied, if you are uncertain, contact an Admin.
  • This is an English-speaking server, you must be able to speak and understand English.
  • All RP must follow Twitch TOS
  • All RP must follow FiveM TOS
  • Please go through proper channels when asking for help & reporting things. For example:
    • Do fill out a Player Report if you feel someone has broken a rule
    • Don't DM Admins with a Player Complaint.
    • Do be sure to include any clips / screenshots of said incident.
    • Don't ping or DM developers telling them something's broken.
    • Do submit a bug report via the bug tracker or use /bug while in-game.
  • All topics of real life politics and religion should be held elsewhere. These topics tend to not end well, so please refrain from discussing them within Hive.

Server Rules

  • Metagaming [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Use of information in-game that was obtained OOC. This includes, but is not limited to, stream-sniping and using 3rd party communications (Ex: Discord)
    • Use of information gained from another one of your characters.
    • Use of information gained from another server.
    • Watching a stream of someone who is playing Hive while you are also playing is prohibited.
  • Breaking Character [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Staying in character is required at all times when in-game.
    • No matter the situation, if someone is breaking rules or something is broken, stay in character.
    • Note: Abuse of /ooc chat in-game is included, please avoid using this if possible.
    • Issues involving rule breaks or any OOC issues should be dealt with out of game.
  • Powergaming [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Unrealistic RP (ex: superpowers, breaking out of cuffs, driving while cuffed.)
    • The abuse of any in-game mechanics and to use it to your advantage.
    • Organizing/Planning while in incapacitated state.
      • Ex: Telling your friends where the cop that downed you is
    • When kidnapping others, you may only rob them for what they currently have. Ex. Taking them to an ATM and forcing them to take money out is not allowed. Please make sure you also have a valid roleplay reason to kidnap someone in the first place, with the intent to rob.
    • Identifying someone by their voice is only allowed if the person is showing more than half of their face
  • No Value of Life (NVL) [Up to 20 Warning Points]
    • You are held at gunpoint, in this scenario, if the aggressor has the advantage, you must cooperate. Pulling out your gun is considered NVL.
    • If outnumbered (3 to 1), pulling your gun is considered NVL.
    • Jumps that are over 3 stories high are considered NVL (this includes dropping into water, jumps on foot AND jumps in a vehicle)
    • Suicide RP is prohibited.
    • After the first few shots have been fired, you are allowed to flee and defend yourself. This means you do not have to stay in place while getting shot.
  • New Life Rule [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • When you are killed and revived by Medics or LEO, you are allowed to remember partial information about the situation. Ex: Colors, Types of Clothing, Facial Features etc.
    • When you are killed and force respawn, you may not remember anything leading up to the cause of your death. This includes colors, name, types of clothing, cars, etc.
    • When you are killed, you may not remember names.
    • You may not return to a scene you were downed at if you force respawn.
  • Medic RP [Up to 10 Warning Points]
    • On-Duty EMS are to remain neutral in all situations. You may not commit any crime against them. This includes kidnapping for a robbery, extortion, murder, etc. (This does not include off-duty EMS)
    • You may not respawn if you have been notified that EMS are en route.
    • Aggressors may not re-initiate after the victim has been revived if intentionally killed by the aggressor.
    • After being revived from a incapacitated state by EMS, you are considered to be in Medical Custody until told otherwise by EMS. While in Medical custody, you may not leave the scene or participate in other crimes.
    • When visiting the Hospital, if medical personnel are available, you must RP with them unless told otherwise by medical staff.
  • Combat Logging [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Logging off to get away from the police.
    • Logging off to not lose gear or items.
    • Logging off when participating in an active RP scenario.
  • Permadeath
    • Force perma (forcing another player to perma kill their character) is not allowed. [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • You are not allowed to use the character again in any other situation unless there is a full server wipe.
    • Permadeath cannot be undone, permadeaths are permanent and cannot be reversed by anyone.
  • Trolling or other attempts to intentionally ruin others experiences is prohibited. [Up to 25 Warning Points]
  • Exploiting/Hacking [Up to 35 Warning Points]
    • Any form of cheating, exploiting or general malicious acts are prohibited.
    • Knowing, but not partaking, is still a bannable offence. If you know of something, report it.
    • Use of any mods/apps/software that give you an advantage over other players. (Tracers, hitmarkers, zoom etc.)
    • Transferring items, cars, money, houses, etc. to your other characters is prohibited (despite having a middleman).

Roleplay Rules

  • 5-Man Rule [Up to 10 Warning Points]
    • Any criminal activities must be limited to 5 people. Anyone participating (spotters, drivers, etc) are included.
    • An exception to this rule is if another party commits a crime against your group, and you already exceed the 5 person limit. In this case, you're allowed to defend yourself until the end of the altercation.
      • The 5-Man rule is reapplied upon reengaging after the initial altercation.
      • Leading someone into your territory to put yourself over 5 is prohibited.
      • Calling for backup after initiation to put yourself over 5 is prohibited.
      • Ex: You and 10 of your friends are hanging out at the Arcade. A group of 5 comes up and attempts to rob you. Yourself and your 10 friends are allowed to defend yourselves.
      • Ex: You and your 4 friends see 8 Cops at the Gas Station and decide to rob them. All 8 cops are allowed to defend themselves.
      • Ex: You and 4 of your friends are running from the police. You arrive at your known hang out spot and 5 of your other friends are there. The 5 people who are already there are not allowed to engage.
      • Ex: You and 4 of your friends are hanging out in your known area. Cops roll up and attempt to arrest you so you decide to shoot at the cops. Calling for more help is not allowed.
  • 6-LEO Rule [Up to 10 Warning Points]
    • There cannot be more than 6 LEOs in any `active` scenario including but not limited to; Robberies, Gun fights, Street Races etc.
    • If 5 out of 6 cops are downed in an active situation, Law Enforcement can call an "All Call", allowing all on-duty officers to respond to the situation
    • If an officer is downed, other officers CANNOT take their place, and would have to wait until the scene is over before trying to help their colleagues. (This rule is overridden by the All Call rule)
    • Air-1 is excluded from this number UNLESS Air-1 lands on scene OR engages in the scene. Air-1 is only allowed to be the “eyes in the sky” to be excluded from this.
    • Off duty LEO are included towards this number
  • Giving out items gained from whitelisted jobs (ex: Police, EMS) is not allowed. [Up to 15 Warning Points]
  • Corruption
    • Officers of the law and lawyers in practice are allowed to be corrupt, to a certain extent.
    • Officers and lawyers are allowed to take bribes to create roleplay (e.g., "Giving money to cops to have them look the other way after speeding, having a lawyer get information through illegal channels that helps your case" etc.).
    • Police officers may NOT sell or give away weapons/items to other players.
    • Anyone in LEO High Command within all departments, IA (International Affairs), EMS High Command and DOJ are considered to have 'powerful' roles and are not allowed to be corrupt.
  • If the job you have signed up for has a job vehicle, you must use it for that job.
    • Ex: Using a personal vehicle to do a job that has a job vehicle is not allowed.
    • Ex: Using a job vehicle to commit a crime is not allowed.
  • Initiation [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Audible AND Visible threats to harm the other party is required
    • Upon initiating, you must give enough time for the other party to react
    • Low Effort Initiation is not permitted. Make your intentions clear and obvious.
      • "There's a baby on your Roof" is Low Effort
      • "Stop or Die" is Low Effort
      • "Hey! Don't Move!" is Low Effort
      • "Don't move, we have guns pointed at you. Don't do anything stupid" is Acceptable.
  • Gang RP
    • Gun fights always require proper initiation, even during war
    • Claiming 'blocks' is allowed, but restricting access to a certain area for no valid roleplay reason is prohibited
    • Taxing is not permitted in any main public criminal locations.
      • Including, but not limited to: Drug manufacture locations, vehicle chop locations, etc.
      • Taxing is possible in any other locations such as gang turf.
  • Neutral Zones [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Pillbox Hospital, Integrity and Mission Row Police Department are considered Neutral Zones. Neutral Zones are areas where crimes are only permitted if you have a VALID roleplay reason.
    • Examples of breaking Neutral Zones
      • Seeing a rival gang go into Pillbox/Integrity, and shooting them when they come out
      • Shooting at MRPD and/or the Officers inside
    • Examples of not breaking Neutral Zones
      • You have a storyline that involves kidnapping someone who snitched on you. Your plan is to take them to an undisclosed location and question them about what they told the cops, but they are standing in front of Integrity and won’t leave.
  • RDM/VDM: Killing other players randomly and/or without a valid RP reason is not permitted. [Up to 35 Warning Points]
    • RDM -> Killing another player for no reason / having no valid role play reason or proper initiation. You must ALWAYS have a valid RP reason to commit harm and or crime against another player.
    • VDM -> Using your vehicle as a weapon for no valid RP reason or proper initiation. (This includes randomly running over NPCs for no reason)
      • The only time using your vehicle as a weapon is acceptable is if someone initiated an attack on you AND you have no other form of defense except for your vehicle.
  • Fail RP: [Up to 20 Warning Points]
    • All RP must be as realistic as possible, if the game physics ruin your day, roll with it.
    • Ex: Jumping off of a building and getting downed and after medical treatment, running away.
    • Ex: Getting into a bad car accident, jumping out of your vehicle and continuing to run.
    • Ex: Downed after a gun fight and trying to escape (running) after you got revived. (Roleplay your injuries!)
  • Low Effort/Quality Roleplay: [Up to 15 Warning Points]
    • Running from the police in every encounter no matter how big or small the situation.
    • Cop Baiting: Intentionally committing crimes in front of police to taunt them. (`I'm trying to give them something to do` is not an excuse!)
    • Using NPCs as hostages.
    • Using friends as hostages. (This includes anyone who is in on the crime) Hostages need to be spontaneous, not staged.
    • Killing NPCs for no valid RP reason.
    • Repeatedly robbing everyone you come across for everything they have (AKA Pocket Wiping)
    • Robbing someone for things you do not need.
    • Chain Robberies (Robbing multiple stores one after another in a small period of time)
    • Intentionally committing crimes 30 minutes before and after server restart.
    • Storing/Retrieving vehicles from garage while in a chase.
    • Using Bennys Garage to repair while in a chase
      • This includes Criminals AND Cops!
    • Entering instanced areas while in a chase (Ex: Apartments and Houses)
    • Use of Hostages to trade for prisoners is prohibited
    • Players who show a habit in violent crime RP, for example, repeated robbing, killing, shooting with little to no valid RP.
    • Cops camping main criminal locations
      • i.e Drug Manufacturing Locations, Vehicle chop locations, etc.
  • Prohibited Roleplay: [Up to 35 Warning Points]
    • Erotic Roleplay (ERP)
    • Suicide RP (Ex: jumping off buildings with a crowd, purposely roleplaying a overdose with the intent to suicide etc.)
    • Extreme Terrorism (Ex: impersonating a suicide bomber etc.)
    • Domestic Violence
    • Rape RP (Immediate removal of the community)
  • Revenge Reporting:
    • Player Reports older than 1 week are deemed insufficient. Please report players ASAP to ensure scenarios are fresh memory. An exception to this if you're gathering evidence to showcase a habit of violent crimes with no valid RP.
    • Reporting someone because they reported you is prohibited.
  • High Command Limitations:
    • If you're ranked LT+ in LSSD, LT+ in LSPD or Trooper+ in SASP you cannot hold a High Command position in DOJ or EMS
    • If you're High Command in EMS (LT+) you cannot hold a High Command position in DOJ, LSSD, LSPD or SASP
    • If you're High Command in DOJ (DA+) you cannot hold a High Command position in EMS, LSSD, LSPD or SASP
  • Body Cam:
    • "Body Cam" is referencing any form of videos that can be used as video evidence within roleplay. Body Cam is only acceptable under these circumstances:
      • If you are NOT LEO or EMS - you must have some sort of camera signifying you are recording
      • If you are LEO and are wearing a bulletproof vest, any body cam is acceptable.
      • If you are LEO and are NOT wearing a bulletproof vest, you must be in close proximity to a patrol vehicle for the body cam to be deemed acceptable.
      • If you are EMS, any body cam is acceptable.
  • Soundboards:
    • Soundboards are only permitted IF used in good taste or to better roleplay.
      • Ex: Playing heart monitor beeps while in the hospital is acceptable.
      • Ex: Playing meme sounds because “it’s funny” is not acceptable.
  • Misc:
    • Robbing a player requires a minimum of 3 cops to be online.
    • Robbing cops inventories is temporarily prohibited while we look into ways to improve pocket wiping issues.
    • Valid RP is defined as having a story line that leads to certain actions.
    • If you are in an active situation and the server goes down or your game crashes, you must make an attempt to communicate with those on the scene and let them know of the situation and/or attempt to return to the scene.
    • Having multiple characters in multiple gangs is prohibited
      • Ex: If your 1st character is in a gang, your other characters cannot join any gangs.
    • Inserting yourself into active situations is prohibited. If you were not apart of the initial situation, do not involve yourself.
      • Exemption: If you are called to assist in the crime after the fact, this rule is not applied.
      • Ex: Running into a bank during an active Bank Robbery.
      • Ex: Running up to an active shootout and robbing downed individuals after a shootout has concluded.
    • Having multiple EMS, LEO, Mechanic or DOJ characters is not allowed.
      • Example: Having 2 EMS Characters
    • Knowing/talking about your other characters is prohibited.
      • Ex: "My cousin is flying in soon"
      • Mention of other people's characters is also prohibited.
        • Ex: "Isn't your cousin a cop?"
      • Exception: You can mention your other characters if they are dead/perma'd.
        • Ex: "Bob Stone" is a cop, but dies in the line of duty, his son, Timmy Stone can be a cop whose father was Bob Stone
