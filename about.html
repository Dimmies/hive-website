ABOUT US

Welcome to Hive! We are a synergic group of individuals from all over the world, working together to design and establish the perfect FiveM Role-Play Community and Server! Our vision and unwavering goal is to provide you with the most enjoyable and satisfying Role-Play experience to date. With the implementation of unique and one-of-a-kind concepts, we aim to make everyone's experience unforgettable. Here, In-Character experiences will thrive, cohesiveness and communication will be indisputable, and Server performance will be breathtakingly reliable. We at Hive pride ourselves in serving you, the Community, to the best of our abilities and will forever be adapting and evolving to continue that promise!

Below you will see the Roster for Hive Staff. Our Staff is composed of the Founders, Developers, Community Managers, Head Admins, Admins, and Moderators who helped bring Hive to life. From countless ideation meetings, to months and months of development, our direction and vision as a team never waivered. All of the Staff that you see below are the ones to applaud for the dedication and determination in the goal to bring you Hive!
