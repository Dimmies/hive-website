Whitelisting Process

You must know and understand the rules, found at this link. Processing may take up to 1-3 Days.

Click on the 'APPLY' button and fill out the application form in its entirety. Be sure to read each section carefully and take your time. Once your application is submitted, our Staff will review your application. If your application is approved, you will receive notice through Discord along with the ‘Whitelisted’ role. If your application is denied, you will receive notice through Discord accompanied with feedback and an explanation on why the application was denied. We give everyone 3 attempts on applying for whitelisting. If your application is denied after the third attempt, you will be able to re-apply after 30 days.
