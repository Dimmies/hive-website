WHITELIST

Application Process
  • You must know and understand the rules, found here
  • Click on the 'Apply' Button and fill out the application form in its entirety. Be sure to read each section carefully. Take your time and be detailed!
  • Once your application is submitted, our Staff will review your application. Processing may take up to 1-3 Days.
  • If your application is approved, you will receive notice through Discord along with the ‘Whitelisted’ role on the Hive Discord.
  • If your application is denied, you will receive notice through Discord accompanied with feedback and an explanation on why the application was denied.
  • We give everyone 3 attempts on applying for Whitelisting. If your application is denied after the third attempt, you will be able to re-apply after 30 days.
