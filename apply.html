Application Process
  • Fill out the application form, don't spare any detail and take your time.
  • Once your application is submitted our staff will review your application, usually within 24 hours.
  • If your application is approved you'll get the Whitelisted role on Discord and we will inform you through Discord.
  • If your application is denied you will receive feedback through Discord and a explanation on why.
  • We give everyone 3 attempts on applying, after these attempts you'll be able to apply again after 30 days.
